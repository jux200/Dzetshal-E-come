About Us

Jardin de Beauté — Redefining Luxury Fragrance in Africa

"Not every perfume is made for everyone… But one is made for you."

Dzetshal is an exclusive luxury perfume retailer with a strong presence in Rwanda and Cameroon. Our mission is to redefine the luxury fragrance experience in Africa by providing 100% authentic, world-class scents from renowned international brands.

We carry exclusive fragrances from Dior, YSL, Giorgio Armani, Chanel, Gucci, Prada, Versace, Dolce & Gabbana and many more — all genuine, all original. Our expert Beauty Advisors help you find your signature scent in-store.

We are proud partners of the Sherrie Silver Foundation, supporting the "Gift Her Confidence" initiative — empowering young women and girls through mentorship and beauty. Visit us today!
